Jewish Passover Apple Cake

Ingredients
6 eggs
2 cups white sugar
1 1/2 cups passover cake meal
1 cup oil (try, canola and vegetable)
7-8 apples - peal and cut up (granny smith)
mix 3/4 cup sugar and cinnamon
pinch salt
1 teaspoon vanilla

Directions
1. cream oil and sugar
2. beat in 1 egg at a time (mix well)
3. add (mix) sifted cake meal
4. grease glass pyrex pan 9x12
5. make a layer of dough and then a layer of apples.
6. Sprinkle cinnamon sugar mixture
7. repeat layer of dough and then a layer of apples, and then a layer of cinnamon sugar mixture
8. end with layer of apples and cinnamon and sugar (no cake meal)
9. bake 350F for 50-55 min
