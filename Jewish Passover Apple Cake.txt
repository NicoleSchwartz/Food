Jewish Passover Apple Cake

Ingredients
* 6 room temp. eggs
* 2 cups white sugar
* 1 1/2 cups sifted passover cake meal
* 1 cup veg oil
* 4 apples - peal and cut up (granny smith)
* cinnamon sugar mix (mix 3/4 cup sugar and 1.5 tsp cinnamon)
* pinch salt
* 1 teaspoon vanilla
* 1/2 stick of melted butter (.25c)

Directions
1. cover apples in melted butter, reserve some to coat pan
2. cream oil, sugar, vanilla and salt
3. beat in 1 egg at a time (mix well, about 1 minute each)
4. add (mix) sifted cake meal (until in ribbons)
5. grease glass pyrex pan 9x13
6. cut parchment paper to cover bottom and sides of pan
7. cover parchmnet with leftover butter
8. pour in half the dough
9. cover in a layer of apples
9. Sprinkle half cinnamon sugar mixture
10. cover with the remaining dough
11. add another layer of apples, then remaining cinnamon and sugar
12. bake 350F for 55 min (golden brown)
