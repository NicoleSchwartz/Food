## Ingredients
* Bread
* Cheese
* Green Chili
* Garlic
* Butter
* red chili flakes
* oregano

## Directions
* toast bread
* slather in butter
* finely chop garlic and chili, top bread with them
* grate cheese, cover toast, melt
* top with spices
* serve with chai
