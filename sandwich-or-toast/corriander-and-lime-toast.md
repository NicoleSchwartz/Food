## Ingredients
* Bread
* peanut butter
* corriander (parsley) chopped leaves
* lime juice
* red chili flakes or cayenne

## Directions
* toast bread
* clather in PB
* sprinkle on seasoning
* sprinkle on lime juice
* top lightly with chopped leaves
