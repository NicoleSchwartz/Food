# Blåbärssoppa

blueberry soup

## Ingredients
* 350 g	(3 cups)	blueberries, fresh or defrosted (or use bilberries if you can)
* 4 tbsp caster (superfine) sugar
* 700 ml (3 cups)	water
* 1½ tbsp potato flour (starch)
