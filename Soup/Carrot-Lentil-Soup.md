Carrot Lentil Soup

## Ingredients
* 1 small bunch carrots
* 1 tsp minced garlic
* 1 tsp minced ginger
* .5-1 tsp lemon herb blend
* .5-1 tsp paprika
* 2 small onions, diced
* 1 veg broth cube
* 1½ cups dried lentils (270g)
* 1 cup milk (I used almond milk, but any other works)
* Salt to taste
* water
* sour cream

### Other ideas
* 2 tablespoons freshly grated ginger
* 2 teaspoons ground cumin
* ¼ teaspoon red chili flakes
* 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
* 2 15- ounce cans diced tomatoes
* 1 cup coconut milk
* 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar, or fresh lemon juice
* 1 tablespoon maple syrup

## Directions
* crock pot, low, everything except lentils, sour cream and milk, just enough water to cover, overnight
* rinse lentils and add next day
* TBD milk, tbd if rice to serve with/on?
* Top with sour cream to serve
