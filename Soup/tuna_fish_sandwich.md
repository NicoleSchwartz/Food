Tuna Sandwich

## Ingredients
* bread
* can of tuna
* mayo

## Ingredients, optional
* cellery
* carrot
* cucumber
* lemon
* letuce
* salt, pepper
* garlic powder
* apples
* pickles
* grapes
* tomatoes
* onion
* capers
* Dijon Mustard
* Cheese, any

## Directions
* drain tuna
* mix tuna, mayo, spices, optional flavors
* toast bread OR if making a tuna melt, put tuna on bread , put on cheese then melt
* put tuna mixture and lettuce, if desired, on bread
