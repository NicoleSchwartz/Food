## Ingredients
* 1 cup sugar
* 1/2 c melted butter
* 2 eggs
* 1/2 c flour
* 1/4 tsp baking powder
* 1/2 tsp salt
* 2/3 c coco powder
* optional: chocolate chunks

## Directions
* mix sugar and butter in a cast iron pan
* add 2 eggs and mix
* add flour, baking powder, salt, coco powder and mix
* optional mix in chocolate chunkls
* 350F for 30 minutes
